How to use the tool
How does Card Trader Manip work?
  • The game initially seeds RNG based on your DS' MAC Address, as well as the date and time set for your DS.
  • The RNG index advances by 4 every time a shooting star appears on the title screen.
  • Once you press New Game, RNG advances approximately 170 times, then seeds your Card Trader.
  • Once seeded, nothing can change what your Card Traders will give you. You can only advance the Card Trader by using one of the 4 Card Traders.
How do I get my MAC Address?
  • Open up your DS, boot up Mega Man Star Force.
  • Either start up a New Game, or Continue your save file.
  • Open the menu with the X button, select ON AIR. You will be forced to save your game.
  • Select ON AIR, then Wi-Fi SETTINGS. You will be forced to save your game.
  • Select Options, then System Information. Your MAC Address will be displayed on the bottom screen.
What are the other fields for?
  • Number of Attempts: Indicates how many times you will use the Card Trader. Keep in mind, you need 3 chips for BIG WAVE, 5 chips for Nacys, 10 chips for Scrapyard, and 10 chhips for AMAKEN per attempt.
  • Cards: Comma-separated list of each card you want to get. For example: "StikyRain1, StikyRain2"
  • Card Trader: The main Card Trader you are looking to use. You can only select one Card Trader at a time (for now).
  • Look for unique cards?: Only check this if you want a single copy of each card in your list. Mostly used for Andromeda Infinity purposes.
How can I test my manip?
  • I recommend first testing your manip using an emulator. To do this, use DesMuMe v0.9.12 (Click on Latest Dev Build).
  • Use the following Lua Script to set your MAC Address on the emulator. You will also need the following text file.
  • Press New Game when the RNG Index reaches the number shown by the tool, minus 170.
  • Your RNG Index should now be 1 greater than the value shown by the tool. If not, you may have to go earlier or later.
  • Once your RNG index is correct, open your folder. Press Select, then Start Over, then press B to let the game pick your favorites. Verify the slots that get favorited are the same as the values listed under "Auto Favorites."
  • Once you've tested with emulator, find a visual cue to help you do the manip without the Lua Script. You can then try testing the manip on your DS.
  • The final thing to test is if you get the cards you expect to. You'll have to play the game up to that point in order to check.
