## Mega Man Star Force 1

### Card Trader Manip Tool

Using this tool, you can specify your DS' mac address, what cards you want, what card trader you want to use, and how many attempts at the card trader you will use. 
The tool will respond with the best manips available for your mac address, as well as what date/time to set on your DS to perform the manip, and what the auto favorites will be to help confirm the manip.
